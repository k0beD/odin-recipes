Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a typical Italian dish made with care in mama mia's kitchen.

It typically consists of tomato sauce, pages of pasta, cheese, and often some minced meat.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Lasagna leaves
  • Garlic
  • Parmeggiano
  • Minced Meat
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Roast Garlic in pan with olive oil.
  2. Add Tomatoes and Basilicum.
  3. Cook for a while while gently stirring. Add minced meat untill cooked.
  4. Take ovendish, layer pasta with sauce, repeat three times.
  5. Sprinkle with Parmeggiano
  6. Cook in oven at 220 C for around 35 min.
